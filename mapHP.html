HIMACHAL TOUR

Day 1: Delhi - Shimla

Assistance on arrival at Delhi Apt. or Railway station & drive directly Shimla. Arrive Shimla & ck in to the hotel Day at leisure & relaxation. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Shimla

Morning after breakfast, sightseeing tour visiting Kufri & Mashobra. Evening fee to explore the Mall Road. Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 3: Shimla - Manali

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Manali. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Evening visit to The Mall road. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Manali

Morning after breakfast, visit the Hadimba Temple and the Tibetan Monastery which houses several images of Lord Buddha and Tankha paintings. Evening is free to relax or stroll and shop at the Mall road. Overnight stay at Manali.

Day 5: Manali (Rohtang Pass/ Solang Valley Excursion)

Morning after breakfast, proceed for a day excursion to the Rohtang pass (Optional) subject to weather condition. Optionally you can visit to Solang Valley, here day at leisure for own activities. Return back to Manali for overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Manali - Dharamshala

Morning after breakfast check out & drive to Dharamshala the principal township of Kangra district which overlooks the wide spread plains with pine and deodar forests. Arrival & ck in to the hotel, post relaxation visit the St. John's Church and the Tibetan market. Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Dharamshala

Morning after breakfast, visit Mc Leodganj - the residence of His Holiness Dalai Lama if permitted. Later time is free for individual activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 8: Dharamshala - Dalhousie

Morning after breakfast check out & drive to Dalhousie; a little hill station that still retains its old British charm. Its marvelous forest trails overlook vistas of wooded hills, waterfalls, spring and rivulets. Arrival & check in to the hotel. Evening at leisure & individual activities. Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 9: Dalhousie

Morning after breakfast, take an excursion to Khajjiar (known as mini Switzerland), a scenic spot set amidst a backdrop of dense pines, deodars and lush green meadows. Time free for individual activities. Return back, evening at leisure & individual activities. Dinner & overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 10: Dalhousie - Amritsar

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Amritsar. Arrival & check in to the hotel. After relaxation visit to Wagha Border, return back to hotel. Evening free for leisure & individual activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 11: Amritsar - Hometown

Morning after breakfast check out, visit to Golden Temple, Jalianwala Bagh & explore the markets around. At time transfer to the Apt. or Railway station for onwards journey. Tour ends.
