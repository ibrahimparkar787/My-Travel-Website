RAJASTHAN TOUR

Day 1: Udaipur

Assistance on arrival at Udaipur Airport / Railway Station and transfer to hotel. After check in & relaxation, if time permits, visit Eklingji & Nagda Temples - built in white marble with a double storied porch on the bank of Lake Bageela - 22 kms from Udaipur. Evening at leisure & individual activities. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 2: Udaipur

After breakfast take a sightseeing tour of Udaipur, visiting City Palace over looking the Lake Pichola, main attraction of the Palace are Durbar Hall, Palace Museum and Jagdish Temple followed by visit to Saheli Ki Bari. Evening at leisure & individual activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Udaipur - Mt. Abu

Morning after breakfast, check out and drive to Mount Abu. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Day at leisure. In the evening you can visit Sunset point and Nakkhi Lake. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Mt. Abu

Morning after breakfast, check out and drive to Mount Abu. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Day at leisure. In the evening you can visit Sunset point and Nakkhi Lake. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Mt. Abu - Jodhpur

Morning after breakfast checkout and drive to Jodhpur, arrive & check in to hotel. Day is free for rest & relaxation. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Jodhpur

After breakfast take a sightseeing tour visiting Clock Tower, Mehrangarh Fort & Jaswant Thada. Day is free for leisure & shopping. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Jodhpur - Manvar

Morning after breakfast checkout and drive to Manvar. Arrive & check in to the Desert Resort. After relaxation full day desert activities. Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 8: Manvar - Jodhpur

Morning after breakfast, at time transfer to the Apt. or Railway station for onwards journey. Tour ends.
