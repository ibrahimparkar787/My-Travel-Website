MAHARASHTRA TOUR

Day 1: Mumbai - Shirdi

Assistance on arrival at Mumbai (Railway station/ Airport) & directly drive to Shirdi, arrive & check in to the hotel. Time is free for darshan & individual activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Shirdi (Excursion Shanishingnapur)

MMorning after breakfast, time for darshan. Afternoon drive to Shanishingnapur time free for darshan. Return back to Shirdi for overnight stay.

Day 3: Shirdi - Aurangabad

Morning after breakfast check out & drive to Aurangabad. Arrive & check in to the hotel. After relaxation visit to Ellora Caves, Daulatabad Fort, Pan Chakki & Bibi Ka Maqbara. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Aurangabad (Excursion to Ajanta Caves)

Morning after breakfast, drive to visit the world famous Ajanta Caves know for its fresco paintings. Return back to the Aurangabad city. Time for leisure & individual activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Aurangabad - Hometown

Morning after breakfast check out & drop at Railway station or Airport for onwards journey.

Optional Extension

Day 5: Aurangabad - Nashik

Morning after breakfast check out & drive to Nashik. Arrive & check in to the hotel. After relaxation, visit to Trimbakeshwar Temple & Panchwati. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Nashik - Mumbai

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Mumbai. Arrive & transfer to the hotel. After check in time for leisure, optionally if time permits visit to nearby places for sightseeing. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Mumbai - Hometown

Morning after breakfast check out & drop to Railway station/ Airport. Tour Ends.
