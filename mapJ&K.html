JAMMU KASHMIR TOUR

Day 1: Jammu - Katra

Assistance on arrival at Jammu Airport or Railway station & drive directly to Katra. Arrival & check in to the hotel. After relaxation day free for relaxation. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Katra

Morning after breakfast, full day free for Vaisnodevi darshan. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Katra - Panitop

Morning after breakfast , check out & drive to Patnitop. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Day free for relaxation. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Panitop - Pahalgham

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Pahalgam. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Time is free to explore the surrounding on own. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Pahalgham

Morning after breakfast, full day free to visit Aru & Chandanwadi (Optional). Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Pahalgham

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Srinagar. Arrive & check in to the hotel. After relaxation take a city sightseeing tour visiting Shree Shankaracharya Temple - dedicated to Lord Shiva Later visit to the Mughal gardens comprising of Chasmashahi, Nishat & Shalimar Gardens (closed on Friday). Evening visit to Dal Lake here you may opt for a Shikara Ride on own. Overnight stay at Srinagar hotel or houseboat.

Day 7: Srinagar (Sonamarg Excursion)

Morning after breakfast, take an excursion to Sonamarg - known for its picturesque snow capped mountains & flower laden Meadows. Here you may opt to hire Ponny on own to visit the Thajiwas Glacier or Yusmarg. Evening return back to the Srinagar. Overnight stay at the hotel or houseboat.

Day 8: Srinagar - Gulmarg

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Gulmarg: situated amidst the snow capped mountains of Pir Panjal Range of the Himalaya is known for its one of the longest and highest ski slopes in Asia. Arrive & Check In to the hotel. After relaxation time free for leisure & to enjoy the surrounding walk ways on own, optionally hire Gondola Ride (on own) & visit to Khilanmarg or other surrounding Ski slopes. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 9: Gulmarg - Srinagar

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Srinagar. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Time is free for individual activities. Evening visit to Dal Lake here you may opt for a Shikara Ride on own. Overnight stay at Srinagar hotel or houseboat.

Day 10: Srinagar - Hometown

Morning after breakfast, check out & drop to Srinagar Airport. Tour Ends.
