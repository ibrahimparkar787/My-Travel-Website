ORISSA TOUR

Day 1: Bhubaneswar

Arrival and Transfer from Apt / Rly stn, Bhubaneshwar is temple town and the temples are just about everywhere. You round a corner and there it is, another temple. You fall asleep and wake up to the ringing of temple bells and chanting of shlokas! If time Permit Half day Visit of Temple. Overnight at Hotel In Bhubaneswar.

Day 2: Bhubaneswar

Full day excursion tour to visit the Nuapatna ( Textile Village ) & Sadeibarini ( Dokra carving village ) Overnight at hotel.

Day 3: Bhubaneswar

Full day visit of Bhubaneswar Temples and Caves. Overnight at hotel.

Day 4: Bhubaneswar - Gopalpur

Morning drive to Gopalpur enroute visit Olasigha Textile village & Chilka lake. Gopalpur is amongst the popular beaches in Orissa. Gopalpur-on-Sea is a beautiful and small beach resort located along the Bay of Bengal in the eastern Indian state of Orissa. Overnight at Gopalpur.

Day 5: Gopalpur - Puri

Morning drive to Puri enroute visit Hirapur ( 64 Yogini Temple , Dhauli & Pipli ).Puri has numerous places of attractions for the tourists in India. Housing two most revered and renown temples of the ancient time, both the places have secured an important place in the tourist map of India. Overnight at Puri.

Day 6: Puri

Full day excursion tour to visit Suntemple( Konark ) and Ragurajput Painting Village. Overnight at Puri.

Day 7: Puri - Bhubaneswar

Departure to Bhubaneshwar and transfer to airport/ railway station.
