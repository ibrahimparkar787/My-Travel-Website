KERALA TOUR

Day 1: Cochin

Assistance on arrival at Cochin airport or railway station & transfer to the hotel. After check in, take a sightseeing tour of the city visiting Jewish Synagogue, Dutch Palace, St. Francis Church & Chinese fishing net. Return back to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 2: Cochin - Munnar

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Munnar. Arrive & check in to the hotel. After relaxation drive to Eravikulam National park. Evening free for relaxation, overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Munnar

Morning after breakfast, visit to Mattupetty Dam, Kundala Lake, Devikulam & Top station. Drive pass the lush green tea plantations & if time permits visit to the Tata Tea Museum. Evening free for leisure & relaxation, overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Munnar - Thekkady

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Thekkady, enroute visit to Kailasam to see the spice plantation & cardamom factory visit. Later continue to drive to Thekkady. Arrive & check in to the hotel. Evening free to explore the spice market & relaxation. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Thekkady - Alleppey

Early morning visit to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, here enjoy a boat cruise on the Periyar Lake to sight the wildlife in the park. Return back to the hotel for breakfast & refreshment. Later check out & drive to Alleppey. Arrive & check in to the houseboat or hotel. Full day free to enjoy this backwaters town. Overnight stay at the houseboat or hotel.

Day 6: Alleppey - Kovalam

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Kovalam. Arrive & check in to the hotel. After relaxation full day free for beach activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Kovalam

Morning after breakfast, drive to visit the Padmanabhaswamy Temple at Trivandrum. Later day free for individual activities. Optionally you can visit to Kanyakumari. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 8: Kovalam - Trivandrum

Morning after breakfast, check out and drop to Trivandrum airport or railway station. Tour Ends.

Extension(Optional)

Day 8 Kovalam - Kanyakumari

Morning after breakfast, check out & drive to Kanyakumari. Arrive & check in to the hotel. After relaxation visit to Kumari Amman Temple, Gandhi Mandapam & take a ferry to visit the Vivekananda Rock Memorial. Later time free for individual activities. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 9: Kanyakumari - Trivandrum

Morning after breakfast, check out & drop to Trivandrum Airport or railway station. Tour Ends.
