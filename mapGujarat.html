GUJARAT TOUR

Day 1: Ahmedabad

Arrive Ahmedabad Airport / Railway station and transfer to hotel. On arrival, check in to the hotel, lunch at hotel.Visit Gandhi Ashram & Akshardham temple. Overnight stay at Ahmedabad.

Day 2: Diu

Breakfast at Hotel and proceed to Diu on the way lunch.Check in to the hotel. The day is at leisure to laze and relax on the beach. Overnight stay at Diu.

Day 3: Diu City tour

Breakfast at Hotel and proceed to Nagoya Beach located in the Nagoa hamlet. The horseshoe beach is exceptionally beautiful and quiet and a wide variety of water sports facilities are available here (water sport activities on direct payment basis) back to hotel, lunch at Hotel.Visit the St. Paul's Church, Diu Museum and the Diu Fort. Overnight stay at Diu.

Day 4: Somnath

MBreakfast at Hotel and proceed to Somnath located on the extreme southwest coast of Gujarat. On arrival, you will visit the temple of Somnath, which is established as one of the twelve Shiva Jyotirlingas. The temple has a tower over 165 feet high over the main sanctum. (Non Hindus are permitted to enter the temple), on the way have lunch. Check in to the hotel. Day at leisure. Overnight stay at Somnath.

Day 5: Sasangirr

Breakfast at Hotel and proceed to Sasangir. On arrival, check in to the hotel, lunch at hotel.Visit the Gir Sanctuary - 11,412 sq km, a hilly tract of mixed dry deciduous and teak forests interspersed with grasslands and home to the Asiatic Lion. One can also spot leopards and other animals like the chital, sambar, nilgai, chausingha, grey langur, hyena and birds like the painted sandgrouse, peacocks and paradise flycatchers. The Kamleshwar Dam Reservoir is habitat to the crocodiles. (The park is closed from 15th June to 15th October).

Day 6: Ahmedabad

Breakfast at Hotel and proceed to Ahmedabad Airport / Railway station for your onward journey.
