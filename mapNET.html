NORTH EAST TOUR

Day 1: Guwahati Airport / Rly station - Tezpur / Bhalukpong

Meet and greet on arrival at Guwahati Airport/ Railway Station and transfer to Tezpur/ Bhalukpong. On arrival check in to the hotel. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Tezpur / Bhalukpong.

Day 2: Tezpur / Bhalukpong - Bomdila

Early morning after breakfast check out from hotel and transfer to Bomdila (8,200 ft.). Check in to the hotel. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Bomdila.

Day 3: Bomdila - Tawang

After breakfast proceed to Tawang (11,500 ft.). Enroute visit Sela Pass (13,700 ft.) & Jaswant Garh. On arrival check in at your hotel. Overnight stay at Tawang.

Day 4: Tawang Sightseeing

After breakfast visit Tawang Monstery, Namgye Lhaste, Pankang Teng Tso Lake (PT Tso Lake), Gorsham Chorten, Gontse Garden, Singar Ani Gompa. Overnight stay at Tawang.

Day 5: Tawang - Dirang

After breakfast proceed to Dirang (4,910 ft.). On arrival check in at your hotel. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Dirang.

Day 6: Dirang - Nameri

After Breakfast transfer to Nameri. Check in the hotel on arrival. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Nameri.

Day 7: Nameri - Guwahati

After Breakfast transfer to Guwahati. Check in at your hotel. In the evening visit 'Kalakshetra', a place where Assamese history and culture is presented through Light and sound show. Overnight stay at Guwahati.

Day 8: Guwahati Airport / Rly Station

After breakfast visit Kamakhya Temple, etc. Transfer to Guwahati Airport / Railway Station for your onward journey.
